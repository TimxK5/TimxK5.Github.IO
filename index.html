This is a whitebox of a subway. I took inspiration from various subways mainly New york and Washingtons. I kitbashed every object to try and create a subway station. It includes ticket machines, a store, lights in the subway, benches, shelves, counter, cash machine, subway card readers, stairs, and subway cars. There is also a view of the buildings outside from the entrance of the subway station.

The End is a vignette inspired by the end of a man's world. The man comes home to see the person he cares for the most betraying him by taking his valuables and attempting to leave him behind. The man makes a desperate action that ends up in regret. The police are called and the man's world comes to an end. He only has two choices. End his life or End his life.

FPS Whitebox2

Whitebox 2 is a custom FPS level. It was made using the FPS starter kit but the level was created by me. It takes place in an underwater jail that an unfortunate error occured and the player can now escape his cell. From there the player will encounter enemies in different rooms while attempting to escape only to realize the only escape is death from chest.
