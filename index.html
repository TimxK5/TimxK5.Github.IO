About

Hello I'm Hiram Sun a Tech Designer, versed in Unreal Engine 4 and Unity. I can program in Blueprints, C++, and Java.

I am currently a student at the University of Central Florida, majoring in Digital Media on the Game Design Track. I have done solo projects and have done collab work with student teams. I also have some experience 3D modeling using Maya. I specialize in programming and implementing game mechanics and also have knowledge about the level designing process.

The Alleyway Disturbance Vignette is a murder scene with a noir style theme. The scene has a person who is sprawled out on the floor dead with another person standing up on a scaffolding, that has broken railings, caused by the man being pushed through them.
